📖 How Some Things Become More Valuable Than Others!

People have used many things as money over time, but not everything makes good money. Here are 8 superpowers that the best types of money have. Let's break them down:

Explore the 8 Attributes of Good Money

Click on each slice of the pie chart below to learn more about each attribute!

Durable 💪

Durable means the money lasts a long time without breaking, rotting, or wearing out. Imagine using bananas as money—they'd squish in your pocket and go bad quickly! But gold or coins? They stay good forever. Bitcoin is super durable because it's digital and can't be destroyed.

Gold coins lasting through time

Portable 🚀

Portable money is easy to carry around. You wouldn't want to lug a heavy rock or a cow everywhere! Coins and bills fit right in your wallet. Bitcoin is the ultimate portable money—you can carry millions of dollars worth on a phone or even memorize it in your head.

Carrying coins in a pocket

Fungible 🔄

Fungible means every unit is the same as another. One $1 bill is exactly like any other $1 bill. If you used unique stickers as money, some might be rarer and worth more. Bitcoin is fungible—every bitcoin is identical to another.

Identical coins

Verifiable ✅

Verifiable means you can easily check if the money is real and not fake. Coins have special marks and weights that are hard to copy. With Bitcoin, the blockchain lets anyone verify transactions instantly without trusting a bank.

Checking a coin's authenticity

Divisible ✂️

Divisible money can be split into smaller parts. A dollar breaks into 100 pennies for small buys. You can't cut a cow in half for a loaf of bread! Bitcoin is divisible down to 8 decimal places, called satoshis.

Breaking a coin into smaller pieces

Scarce 🌟

Scarce means there's a limited amount, so it keeps its value. If money grew on trees, it wouldn't be worth much! Gold is scarce because it's hard to find. Bitcoin has a fixed supply of 21 million coins—no more can be made.

Rare gold nugget

Has a Long History 📜

Money with a long history is trusted because people have used it for ages. Gold has been money for thousands of years! Bitcoin is newer, but it's building a strong history as digital gold.

Ancient gold coins

Censorship-Resistance 🛡️

Censorship-resistant money can't be easily stopped or seized by others, like governments. If a bank freezes your account, you can't use your money. Bitcoin is resistant because it's decentralized—no one controls the whole network.

Secure digital wallet
