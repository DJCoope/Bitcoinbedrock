📘 What is Money?

Let’s explore where money came from and why it’s important!

Money: A Super Helpful Tool

Before money, people used to trade things directly. This was called barter. For example, you might trade a banana for a sandwich. But what if the other person didn’t want a banana?

That’s where money came in. People needed something everyone agreed was valuable, easy to carry, and could be used to trade for anything.

💡 Fun Fact: The first "money" was often salt, shells, or shiny stones!

What Makes Good Money?

Good money should be:

  • 🧮 Easy to count
  • 👜 Easy to carry
  • 👶 Last a long time
  • 🌍 Accepted by many people

Later, people started using gold and silver. Then came paper money. And now... digital money like Bitcoin!

💡 Can you imagine using candy or marbles as money? Some kids did that on playgrounds!
