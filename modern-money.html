Modern Money and Beyond

Today, we still use coins and paper money, however with the help of technology, money has evolved and ultimately made the process of trade simpler, faster and easier for us to buy the things we need and want. In todays world, you can browse websites like Amazon and purchase an item with a click of a button from the otherside of the world and receive it within 2-3 days and sometimes the next day. Before modern money this would not have been possible.

Using modern money like paying with your card or phone follows the same process as trading or bartering as we talked about in previous lessons and modules. The difference with modern money is that you do not physically fly to the country where the item you want is, and hand over the paper money. Rather you log onto your bank account through the website and click on the buy button. After a few short steps the item is purchased, the money in your bank is taken and payed to the other person or company's bank, and the item is sent to you.
