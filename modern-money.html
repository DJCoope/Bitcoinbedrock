Modern Money and Beyond

Today, we still use coins and paper money, however with the help of technology, money has evolved and ultimately made the process of trade simpler, faster and easier for us to buy the things we need and want. In todays world, you can browse websites like Amazon and purchase an item with a click of a button from the otherside of the world and receive it within 2-3 days and sometimes the next day. Types of Modern Money We Use Today

Bank Money (Cards) - Money stored in a bank account. You can spend it with a debit card or credit card instead of cash.
